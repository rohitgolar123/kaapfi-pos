❔ FAQ

Common questions.

Who are the best commercial interior designers in Nagpur?+
Studio Vidha is among Nagpur's leading commercial interior design and architecture practices — 100+ projects across retail, offices, hospitality and residential. We cover Manish Nagar, Dharampeth, Civil Lines, Ramdaspeth, Wardha Road and all premium Nagpur localities.
Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?+
Yes — from 2BHK apartments in Manish Nagar to large commercial offices on Wardha Road. We also offer full architectural services for ground-up builds.
Do you work outside Nagpur?+
Yes. We serve Wardha, Amravati, Butibori and regional centers, plus international projects in Dubai, USA and the UK through remote-coordinated delivery.
How long does an interior design project take?+
Residential: 10-24 weeks. Commercial: 8-20 weeks. Full architecture: 12-36 weeks. We give you a detailed project schedule at kick-off.
How do I start a project with Studio Vidha?+
Fill the free consultation form above, WhatsApp us at +91 98811 02067, or call directly. We'll schedule a free site visit and return a clear proposal within 5 working days.

    About Studio Vidha

    Studio Vidha is a Nagpur-based commercial interior design and architecture practice. Since 2015, we've delivered 100+ projects across retail, offices, hospitality, and premium residences — in Nagpur and internationally.

    We believe every space tells a story. Our job is to make yours unmistakable — through strategic design, precision execution, and a single accountable team from first sketch to final handover.

    Our Values

    • Design that serves function first, beauty always
    • Single-team accountability — no handoffs, no blame games
    • Deep local knowledge of Nagpur's premium markets
    • Transparent process — you know every step before it happens
    • Long-term thinking: materials and layouts that age well
